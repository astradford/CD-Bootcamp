UpcycledEats

Diverting just 15% of currently wasted food would reduce the number of food insecure Americans by 50%.*

*Source: USDA, https://www.ers.usda.gov/topics/food-nutrition-assistance/food-security-in-the-u-s/


Upcycled Eats partners with grocery stores and restaurants to offer an easy alternative to dumping produce that also serves the community. Instead of discarding unused produce, Upcycled Eats releases liability of all products, and will facilitate pickup, sorting/filtering, and delivering with vetted organizations and agencies that help those in need.

43 BILLION pounds of food is wasted annually by grocery stores (NRDC)
Increasing state legislation requiring grocers to donate and limit dumping.
Upcycling equals less food in the landfills, reducing our carbon footprint.
